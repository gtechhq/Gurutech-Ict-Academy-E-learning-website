About Our Gurutech Ict Academy

Welcome to GuruTech ICT Academy , where the future of technology is shaped through a comprehensive and immersive 3-year training program. At GuruTech, we believe in providing students with a unique educational experience that goes beyond traditional computer science or software engineering curricula offered in public or private schools..

Vision: Our vision at GuruTech ICT Academy is to empower the next generation of tech leaders by offering a specialized and in-depth 3-year training program in technology, software development, and computer skills.

Program Structure: Unlike conventional educational paths where students choose to major in computer science or software engineering, GuruTech's program is designed to provide a holistic and integrated approach. Students engage in a dynamic curriculum that covers a wide range of tech skills, ensuring a well-rounded understanding of the ever-evolving tech landscape.
